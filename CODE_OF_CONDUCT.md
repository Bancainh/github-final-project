## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include using welcoming and inclusive language.

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Community leaders are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies both within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Community leaders will follow these Community Impact Guidelines.
